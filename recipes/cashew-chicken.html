Cashew Chicken Recipe

Recipe

Ingredients

  • Cashews
  • Cornstarch
  • Chicken Breast
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375
  2. Bake cashews until fragrant
  3. Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper and fry for 5 minutes
  4. Cook garlic, vegtables, and seasonings until brown and add rice
  5. Add the sauce to the rice and chicken and serve warm
